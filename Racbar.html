About

We hereby introduce the RACBAR Multidynamics Limited Center and invite you and your team to pay a collaboration/learning and support visit to our Ibadan Sport and Recreation Center respectively to intimate you with our unique Sporting, Training, Fitness and personal development services which complements the efforts of your Youth and sport development in ensuring that the society is free from violence, crime and other social vices which the use of sport as a veritable tool will help address.

Our services address the identified gaps involved in the prevention and response to youth development, sexual abuse and unemployment in Nigeria, and ensure access to quality care, protection and support.

The Foundation also supports the revision of discriminatory laws, regulation policies, systems and practices at national and local levels, as well as the development and implementation of a gender/child friendly policy within the Nigerian Police to enhance the protection of survivors of child sexual abuse and their non-offending families.
