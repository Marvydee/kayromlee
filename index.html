About The CEO

In the tapestry of her life, Ambassador Ayinde Rahmat Romoke stands as a beacon of success and philanthropy. From her ventures in Agriculture, Sport, Hospitality, and Fashion to her role as a UN Peace Ambassador, her journey is marked by accolades and commendations. She's not just a businesswoman; she's a force behind Kayrom Lee Group of Companies, comprising Kayrom Lee Farm, Gym & Fitness Centre, Travel & Tours, Fashion & Beauty Centre, and Lee Kut Garment Factory. Recognition follows her, from the Presidency to Sport Writers Association, honoring her for contributions to tennis and sports in Oyo State.
